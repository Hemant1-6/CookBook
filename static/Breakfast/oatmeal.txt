PREP TIME:
0 HOURS 10 MINS

INGREDIENTS
1/2 c. plus 2 tsp. milk, divided
1 c. old-fashioned rolled oats
1 tsp. ground cinnamon, divided
3 tbsp. powdered sugar


DIRECTIONS
In a medium saucepan over high heat, bring milk to a boil. Stir in oats and 1/2 tsp. 
cinnamon, reduce heat to low, and simmer until oats are tender and creamy, 5 minutes.

Make icing: In a small bowl, whisk together remaining 2 tsp. milk and powdered sugar.

Serve oatmeal in a bowl drizzled with icing and sprinkled with remaining 1/2 tsp. cinnamon.
